About

RUclassboard offers several features:

  • Classboards are created by unique names

Users are required to connect to a class by typing in a keyword to identify with the course. After entering in the keyword, all posts related to the keyword will display.

  • All posts are tracked with times

Posts are ordered by time for easy viewing. Newest posts are shown at the top and each post displays the time.

  • Each classboard contains an activity area to show various class statistics

Total posts and unique users will be shown.

  • Accounts are password protected

Users can only access RUclassboard through an account which is protected by a password.
